# Post-Submit Approval Checklist

Goal: maximize approval probability while staying fully accurate.

## Within 24 hours

1. Watch inbox + spam for Anthropic messages.
2. Keep GitHub profile and account email stable.
3. Check tracked PRs for activity: `#315`, `#316`, `#317`, `#49`, `#320`, `#664`, `#123`, `kana-dojo#6309`, `grafana#119212`.

## Daily for next 7 days

1. Respond to maintainer feedback within 24 hours.
2. If feedback arrives, push minimal targeted updates only.
3. Update `oss-maintenance-log` with new commits, comments, or state changes.
4. Review `evidence/action-queue.md` and clear oldest pending items first.

## Weekly

1. Add one small high-signal maintenance action (fix, triage, or docs correction).
2. Keep all evidence linkable and public.

## Do / Don't

Do:
- Keep every claim verifiable by public links.
- Acknowledge compatibility and tradeoffs in PR descriptions.
- Keep diffs small and reviewable.

Don't:
- Exaggerate maintainer role or ecosystem impact.
- Open noisy PRs without issue linkage.
- Change identity details right after applying.
