# Post-Submit Approval Checklist

Goal: maximize approval probability while staying fully accurate.

## Within 24 hours

1. Watch email inbox + spam for Anthropic messages.
2. Keep GitHub profile and email stable (no username/email churn).
3. Check PRs `#315`, `#316`, `#317` for reviewer activity.

## Daily for next 7 days

1. Respond to maintainer feedback within 24 hours.
2. If feedback arrives, push minimal targeted updates (no scope creep).
3. Update `oss-maintenance-log` with any new commits or review outcomes.

## Weekly

1. Add one small high-signal maintenance action (fix, triage, doc correction).
2. Keep all evidence linkable and public.

## Do / Don’t

Do:
- keep statements in application verifiable with links
- acknowledge tradeoffs and compatibility in PR descriptions
- keep change sets small and reviewable

Don’t:
- exaggerate maintainer role or project impact
- open noisy PRs without clear issue linkage
- change identity details right after applying
